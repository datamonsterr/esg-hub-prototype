How Cascading Works

Required Cascading: Your Tier 1 suppliers must distribute this assessment to their selected lower tier suppliers as a compliance requirement.

Optional Cascading: Tier 1 suppliers can choose to cascade the assessment to lower tiers at their discretion.

Material/Product Specific: Only suppliers directly involved in production or sourcing of specific materials will receive the assessment.

Risk-based Selection: The system automatically identifies high-risk suppliers based on geographic location, processes, and previous assessment scores.
